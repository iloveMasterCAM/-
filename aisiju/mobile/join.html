Assistant architect

Job Responsibilities

1. is responsible for the implementation of relevant standards, specifications and design requirements, to complete the design task, ensure design quality;

2. According to the project master plan, complete professional design stage will report and make recommendations solutions that ensure the design project on schedule to complete the task;

3. According to the project situation, and active cooperation with the cooperation unit to ensure the smooth flow of information, timely feedback;

4. With the sales department to complete the technical program, participate in the project bidding work;

5. With the procurement department to complete the design process of equipment selection, to provide technical parameters of materials for the procurement process of technical issues check;

6. Deal with the problems arising from the course of the construction process, promptly propose a solution, timely with the site guidance, and lessons learned.

Conditions of employment

1. Bachelor 's degree in engineering and construction, 5 years of experience in construction - related design experience in large design institute background or large - scale engineering design experience is preferred;

2. architectural design and construction details are familiar;

3. Skills Certificate: Registered Architectural Engineer (Level 1 or 2 Certificate); Proficiency in CAD and other design software, a comprehensive grasp of architectural design knowledge, domestic and international construction industry, the latest technology and norms;

4. Have time management and self - management ability, have good communication and expression ability, love and dedication, recognition of corporate culture;

5. Have the ability to solve complex problems, independent work ability, work ability and strong execution force; Good English reading and writing skills, good team spirit and coordination ability.
